Major wars in world history

World War 1
  • Duration: 4 years (1914-1918)

  • War related Deaths: 16-22 million people

  • Wounded: 21 million people

  • Countries involved: Over 100 countries

  • Soldiers lived in trenches

  • The consequences of the war led to WW2

  • An epidemic of influenza was created due to all the travelling, the epidemic ended up killing 25 million people

  • Massive inflation followed the war and the economy of the losers plummeted
World War 2
  • Duration: 6 years (1939-1945)

  • War Related Deaths: 50-56 million people

  • Cost of the war (adjusted to todays inflation): over 4 trillion dollars

  • Countries involved: 23 countries

  • War-related Disease and famine ravaged people at the time and an additional 28 million people died because of it taking the total amount of deaths due to the war to 70-85 million people

  • The end of the war, most importantly defeat of the Germans also marked the end of the Holocaust, one of the worst events of discrimination in human history
Vietnam War
  • Duration: 20 years (1955-1975)

  • War Related Deaths: over 3.6 million people

  • Cost of the war (adjusted to todays inflation): 168 billion- 1 trillion dollars

  • Countries involved: 8 countries

  • During the war most soldiers that participated for vietnam were volunteers

  • The war left vietnam
